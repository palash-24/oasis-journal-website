Our Story

Meet Neha

Oasis Journals was founded by Neha Shyamdasani in 2024 after her personal journey with journaling transformed her mental health and productivity.

"I started journaling during a difficult time in my life, and it became my daily oasis of calm. I wanted to create beautiful, purposeful journals that would help others find that same peace."
